# FUTURE_DS_02
Social Media Campaign Performance Tracker – Future Interns

## Key Insights

- The campaign generated strong overall engagement with 79M impressions and 12K clicks.
- The age group **30–34** delivered the highest number of clicks, indicating strong ad resonance with this demographic.
- **Male users** contributed more clicks than female users, showing better performance in male targeting segments.
- Certain interest categories, especially those at the top of the interest1 list, produced significantly higher engagement.
- Campaign spending shows varying efficiency — some campaigns deliver higher conversion value compared to spend.
- High ROI of **838%** indicates that conversions greatly outweigh advertising investment.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus future ad spend on age group **30–34**, as it consistently drives more engagement.
- Increase targeting toward **male audiences** where performance metrics are stronger.
- Scale winning interest categories to further boost reach and conversions.
- Optimize budget allocation by reducing spend on lower-performing campaigns.
- Experiment with new creatives and CTAs to improve CTR among weaker audience segments.
